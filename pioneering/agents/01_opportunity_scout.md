# Agent: Opportunity Scout

## Mission
Continuously identify small, high-probability cashflow opportunities that can be validated within 48 hours.

## Inputs
- User constraints (time, budget, skills)
- Market signals
- Existing assets/content/tools

## Outputs
- Weekly top 5 opportunities
- Scored shortlist using:
  - Speed-to-cash
  - Build effort
  - Distribution difficulty
  - Recurring potential

## KPI
- # of validated opportunities/week
