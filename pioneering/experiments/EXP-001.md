# EXP-001 — First Cashflow in 7 Days

## Hypothesis
A practical digital product can generate first paid conversion in <= 7 days with lightweight distribution.

## Selected Offer
**WhatsApp Business Reply Kit (Arabic + English)**
- 120 pre-written message templates for SMBs
- Categories: inquiry replies, follow-ups, payment reminders, complaint handling, escalation, out-of-stock, upsell

## Audience
- Small business owners in Bahrain/GCC
- Home businesses, service freelancers, small shops

## Price Tiers
- Lite: BHD 4 (30 templates)
- Pro: BHD 8 (80 templates)
- Business: BHD 12 (120 templates + editable placeholders)

## Success Criteria (48h)
- >= 30 qualified leads reached
- >= 10 intent-positive responses
- >= 1 paid conversion

## Decision Rule
- GO: 1+ paid conversion within 48h
- PIVOT: high interest, no payment
- KILL: low interest and no conversion
