# Follow-up Cadence (Simple)

## T+0 (initial DM)
Use: `publish/OUTREACH_DM.md`

## T+6h (no reply)
"Quick follow-up — want me to send a free sample before you decide?"

## T+24h (interested, not paid)
"I can reserve launch price today. Want Lite, Pro, or Business?"

## T+48h (soft close)
"Last follow-up from me 🙏 If helpful, I can send a sample and you can decide later."

## Rule
Stop after 3 follow-ups unless they engage.
